Raw fish and rice

Description

Sushi is a Japanese dish featuring vinegared rice combined with various ingredients such as raw fish, vegetables, and seaweed, often served with soy sauce and wasabi.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori (seaweed)
  • Fresh fish (like tuna or salmon)

Steps to prepare

  1. Prepare the Sushi Rice: Rinse sushi rice until the water runs clear, then cook according to package instructions. Once cooked, mix with rice vinegar, sugar, and salt, then let it cool to room temperature.
  2. Prepare the Fillings: Slice fresh fish and other desired fillings (like cucumber or avocado) into thin strips.
  3. Assemble the Sushi Rolls: Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat. Spread a thin layer of sushi rice over the nori, leaving a border at the top. Arrange the fish and other fillings in a line across the rice. Roll the nori tightly using the mat, then slice the roll into bite-sized pieces with a sharp knife. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
