Description

Lasagna is an Italian pasta dish made with layers of lasagna noodles, rich meat or vegetable sauce, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella, baked until bubbly and golden.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Ground beef (or Italian sausage)

Steps to prepare

  1. Prepare the Sauce: In a skillet, cook ground beef or Italian sausage until browned. Add garlic, onions, and a jar of marinara sauce. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Prepare the Cheese Mixture: In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg, grated Parmesan cheese, and chopped parsley.
  3. Assemble the Lasagna: Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of lasagna noodles. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, then sprinkle with shredded mozzarella. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of meat sauce and mozzarella on top.
  4. Bake: Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
