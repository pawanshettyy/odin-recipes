Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with various ingredients such as seasoned meat, vegetables, and salsa

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Ground beef (or other preferred meat)
  • Salsa

Steps to prepare

  1. Prepare the Filling: In a skillet, cook ground beef until browned. Add taco seasoning and a bit of water, then simmer until the meat is fully cooked and the seasoning is well incorporated.
  2. Warm the Tortillas: Heat tortillas in a dry skillet for about 30 seconds on each side or until they are warm and pliable.
  3. Assemble the Tacos:Place the seasoned beef on each tortilla, then top with salsa, shredded lettuce, cheese, and any other desired toppings like guacamole or sour cream. Fold and serve immediately.
