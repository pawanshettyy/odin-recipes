Description

Butter Chicken, also known as Murgh Makhani, is a popular Indian dish made of tender chicken pieces cooked in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce with aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tomato puree
  • Cream

Steps to prepare

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, mix chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, and spices (such as turmeric, cumin, and chili powder). Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the Chicken: Heat oil in a pan and cook the marinated chicken until it's browned on all sides. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. Prepare the Sauce: In the same pan, add butter and saut√© onions until golden. Add tomato puree, spices (garam masala, coriander, and fenugreek), and cook until the oil separates from the sauce. Add cream and mix well.
  4. Combine and Simmer: Add the cooked chicken to the sauce and simmer for 15-20 minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with naan or rice.
